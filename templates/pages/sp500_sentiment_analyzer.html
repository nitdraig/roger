S&P 500 Sentiment Analysis?

This analysis helps you understand how the market feels about the S&P 500 index, based on the latest news. Here's what you need to know: Average Sentiment Score: This is a number that tells us whether the news is generally positive or negative about the S&P 500. A high number means the news is positive. , and a low number means they are negative. Sentiment: Tells you if the general sentiment is positive, negative or neutral, based on all the news analyzed. Total Number of Articles: Shows how many news items have been reviewed to calculate sentiment. In summary, This analysis gives you a quick view of how the market is doing based on the latest news, without you having to read all the news yourself.
