What is the Investment Calculator?

The Investment Calculator helps you estimate the return on your investments. This is how it works: You enter your Initial Investment: You enter how much money you plan to invest at the beginning. You Set the Investment Time: You indicate how long you plan to keep your investment. Choose the Expected Rate of Return: You select a rate of return, which is the percentage you expect to earn on your investment. Get the Result: The calculator will show you how much your investment could grow over time, based on the data you entered. Simply put, it helps you see how your money could grow over time, helping you better plan your investments.
